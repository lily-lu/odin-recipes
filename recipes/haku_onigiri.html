Haku's Onigiri

Description

From the Studio Ghibli film Spirited Away, Chihiro's friend, Haku, gives her a Japanese rice ball known as onigiri. Onigiri is one of the most popular foods in Japan and is typical Japanese soul food. It is commonly triangular shaped and is wrapped in dried seaweed called nori. A variety of fillings exist where the most common include tuna mayo, salmon, and pickled plum.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups cooked Japanese rice
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp sesame seeds (black or normal works)
  • salt to taste
  • 1 sheet of nori
  • 1 can of tuna
  • 2 tbsp Japanese mayo
