Ponyo's Ramen

Description

From the Studio Ghibli film Ponyo, a memorable scene of the characters having ramen is depicted. Being the comfort food of many, instant ramen is deeply embedded in the food culture of countries such as Japan and South Korea. The ease in which it can be made to the extensive variety of flavors makes instant ramen widely popular and strongly loved.

Ingredients

  • 1 instant ramen noodle packet
  • 1 egg hard or soft boiled
  • 1 green onion sliced
  • 1 piece ham cut in half
  • 2.5 cups water
